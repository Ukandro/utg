Employment

| |

Over 2 million people are employed in the manufacturing of tobacco products, two thirds of whom are working in China, Indonesia and India. Some people see tobacco control efforts as impacting on tobacco jobs, yet the main source of impact on people‟s jobs is the technological advances in both manufacturing and farming, which allow for a lower number of employees.
